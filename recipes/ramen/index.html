Ramen Recipe

Procedure

  1. Take 1 onion and chop it into small pieces.
  2. Cut 1 slice of cheese into smaller parts.
  3. Take a few cloves of garlic and cut them into smaller pieces.
  4. In a pan, fry the chopped onion and garlic with oil until caramelized. Add the cheese parts.
  5. Add water to the pan as per your needs.
  6. Add Maggi noodles and the accompanying masala to the pan.
  7. Add a pinch of common salt.
  8. Add pizza flavorings that come with pizza.
  9. Cook on medium flame until the noodles are cooked.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Ramen.
