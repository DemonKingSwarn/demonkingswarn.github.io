Meat on a Bone Recipe

Ingredients

  • Few chicken drumsticks
  • Rice paper
  • Boiled eggs

Procedure

  1. Take a few chicken drumsticks.
  2. Cut them along the bone handle, but do not completely cut them.
  3. Put a boiled egg inside the drumsticks.
  4. Seal the drumsticks with rice paper.
  5. Fry them until golden brown.
