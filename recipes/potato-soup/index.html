Potato Soup Recipe

Procedure

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Grate 1 potato and add it to the boiling water.
  3. Add 2 slices of cheese and a pinch of salt.
  4. Add pizza flavourings and stir well.
  5. Extract the soup into a bowl.
  6. Add potato chips as a topping.
  7. Enjoy your delicious potato soup!
