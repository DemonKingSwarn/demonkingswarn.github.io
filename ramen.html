• Take 1 onion.
  • Chop the onion into small pieces.
  • Take 1 slice of cheese and cut it in smaller parts.
  • Take few cloves of garlic and cut them in smaller pieces.
  • Fry the onion and garlic in a pan with oil, add cheese parts when they get caramalized.
  • Add water as per your needs.
  • Add maggi noodles to it and its masala.
  • Add 1 pinch of common salt.
  • Add pizza flavourings (comes additionally with pizza).
  • Cook in medium flame.
  • Serve and enjoy.
